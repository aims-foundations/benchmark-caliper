[Input Form — Missing Concerns]

I would focus more on the content and less on stylistic genre-convention: "(numbered bullets, standardized headers, parroquia-level
damnificado figures)". In other words, if the content is there in the output summary, a follow up stage could focus on reformatting the output.
As a minor comment, I believe the term "parroquia-level damnificado" is an hallucinations or super specific example that I would not expect in this type of document

I believe this assessment and all the documents would be highly useful as a starting point of discussion/analysis for interdisciplinary roundtables.
