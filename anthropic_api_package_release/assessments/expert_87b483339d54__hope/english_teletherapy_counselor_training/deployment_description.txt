Use case and domain: A conversational AI system for mental health counseling classifies dialogue acts in real-time counseling conversations using the HOPE benchmark and any classification model. The system identifies the communicative intent (e.g.,  question, clarification) of each utterance by counselors and clients, and subsequently allows downstream tasks such as session quality assessment, automated counselor feedback, and training support in clinical and teletherapy settings.
Target population: Mental health counselors, trainee therapists, and clinical supervisors who conduct or review online counseling sessions in English. This includes practitioners and researchers working with teletherapy platforms and peer support services who need automated tools for counseling quality evaluation and dialogue understanding.
