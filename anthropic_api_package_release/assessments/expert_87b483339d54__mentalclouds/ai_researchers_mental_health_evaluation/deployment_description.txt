Use case and domain: Researchers and developers building or evaluating AI-driven counseling support tools assess the capability of LLMs to produce faithful, coherent, and clinically appropriate summaries from counseling dialogues. The benchmark provides a standardized evaluation framework to compare multiple LLMs across summarization quality metrics relevant to the mental health domain.
Target population: NLP researchers and AI practitioners in South Asia and the Middle East and North Africa who are working on applied clinical NLP, digital mental health platforms, and LLM evaluation for low-resource or underrepresented languages and communities.
